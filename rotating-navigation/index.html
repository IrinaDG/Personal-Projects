Travelling while working

Laura Dean

In this week's article we will approach the theme travel&work. The dream of almost any millenial, the idea of working and discovering the world started to become more and more readily accessible to everyone. With the job market on a continuous development, it was only a matter of time until remote work could be done accross a large variety of job fields. This week we will discover the story of Sam, who is basically living the dream with his remote job in marketing.
